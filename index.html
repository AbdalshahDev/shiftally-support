What it does

Your hours, on the record

Every shift you work should count — and add up. ShiftTally keeps a clean, private record of your hours and what they're worth.

Log shifts in seconds

Start time, end time, done. Add breaks if you took them. ShiftTally handles the rest — no spreadsheets, no mental arithmetic at the bus stop.

Automatic pay calculation

Set your hourly rate once and every shift is priced instantly. See your daily, weekly and monthly earnings — and catch payroll mistakes before they cost you.

Summaries at a glance

Weekly and monthly totals show your hours and earnings in one clean view. Perfect for checking your payslip actually matches what you worked.

Private by design

No sign-up, no account, no cloud. Your shifts live on your device and work completely offline. Your hours are nobody's business but yours.
