Support

How can we help?

Answers to common questions about ShiftTally. Can't find what you need? Email us — we read everything.

How do I log a shift?

Tap the add button on the main screen, enter your start and end time, add a break if you took one, and save. Your shift appears in your log straight away with the pay calculated automatically.

How do I set my hourly rate?

Go to Settings and enter your hourly rate. Every shift you log is calculated using this rate. If your rate changes, update it in Settings — shifts you've already logged keep the rate they were logged with.

Can I edit or delete a shift?

Yes. Tap any shift in your log to open it, then edit the times or delete it. Your totals update automatically.

Where is my data stored? Is it private?

All of your shifts are stored locally on your device. ShiftTally has no accounts, no cloud sync and no ads, and it works completely offline. We can't see your data — see our Privacy Policy for full details.

Will my shifts transfer to a new phone?

Because your data is stored on-device, it moves with a full device transfer or restore from an iPhone backup (iCloud or computer backup). Deleting the app deletes your shift data, so back up your device before removing the app.

Does ShiftTally support overtime or multiple jobs?

We're actively working on new features based on what users ask for — including export and multiple jobs. If there's something you need, email us and tell us. Requests genuinely shape what gets built next.
